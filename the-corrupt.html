James Langley is searching for the man who stole his life. Three long years after he was forced to abandon his hometown of Eugene, he and his ghostly companion Virgil, still hunt the wicked Doctor Edwin Burke. Granted superhuman power by The Shade—a plain of existence between life and death—they protect the world of humans against the nightmares that lie below. However, when Burke resurfaces, and a growing number of Shade dwelling creatures begin to surround the city; James and Virgil are called back to defend the quiet city of Eugene. Though the motive for the gathering is unknown to them, James believes it to be a sign that The Speakers Burke heralded, are organizing their forces for war. Marred by his past and haunted by the growing darkness within him, James must return to protect his home from the coming tide of vicious monsters. Get ready for the supernatural sequel to The Shade. A world where you fight the shadows, or are consumed by them.
